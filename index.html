About Me

Hello, my name is Esma Gadzo, I'm a UX Designer. I am a passionate, hard working, and creative individual that loves to create perfect UX Design.

I think UX design is more than making things pretty, UX design can influence and manipulate people. What I enjoy about UX Design is that it allows me to take complex problems and turn them into smaller digestible pieces. Along with that I like how you play many different roles as a UX Designer. For example, as a UX designer I am also a psychologist and researcher.

I am talented at many different UX tasks however user research is my strongest area of competence. My undergraduate studies in Political Science have greatly advanced my research skills.
